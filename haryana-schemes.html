Haryana Government Schemes for MSMEs & Textiles

A comprehensive summary of key policies and schemes notified by the Government of Haryana for MSMEs and the textile sector.

I. Haryana Micro Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) Policy-2019

This policy aims to augment MSME competitiveness, create infrastructure, foster innovation, and align with national initiatives. It builds on the Haryana Enterprise Promotion Policy (EPP) 2015.

  • Cluster Plug and Play Scheme: Up to 50% grant (max ₹5 Cr) for flatted factories on 5+ acres. Valid till Feb 2024.
  • State Mini Revamped Scheme Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SMSFURTI): 90% grant (up to ₹3 Cr for Heritage, ₹1.5 Cr for Major, ₹0.75 Cr for Mini Clusters). Valid till Feb 2024.
  • State Renewable Energy Scheme: 5% interest subsidy on term loans (max ₹10 lakh/year for 3 years) for renewable energy equipment. Valid till Feb 2024.
  • State Mini Lean Manufacturing Competitiveness Scheme: 80% assistance (max ₹2 lakh/unit) for lean manufacturing consultancy. Min 8 units per cluster. Valid till Feb 2024.
  • SME Exchange Equity Scheme: 20% reimbursement (max ₹5 lakh) for SME exchange listing expenses. Valid till Feb 2024.
  • Reimbursement of Electricity Duty Payment: 100% reimbursement for 7 years for new MSMEs in all blocks. Valid till Feb 2024.
  • Skill Development & Training: 50% reimbursement (max ₹5,000/worker/program) for MSME worker training. Valid till Feb 2024.
  • Refund of Stamp Duty Scheme: 100% (D), 75% (C), 50% (A/B) refund for land purchase/lease for Micro & Small Enterprises. Valid till Feb 2024.
  • Promotion of ICT Scheme for MSE Sector: 75% subsidy (max ₹3 lakh for 2 years) for cloud ERP adoption. Valid till Feb 2024.

II. Haryana AatmaNirbhar Textile Policy 2022-25

This policy promotes value-added textile activities, investments, and employment, aiming to make Haryana a global textile hub.

  • Textile Park Scheme: 40% grant (max ₹20 Cr) for infrastructure in Textile Parks. 100% stamp duty reimbursement and IDC exemption. Max 10 parks. Valid till 2025.
  • Promoting Green and Sustainable Production: 50% assistance (max ₹5 Cr for A/B, ₹10 Cr for C/D) for ETP/ZLD; 50% (max ₹20 lakh) for solar rooftops. Valid till 2025.
  • Textile Cluster Development Scheme: 90% grant (max ₹10 Cr) for CFCs in textile clusters (min 15 units). Valid till 2025.
  • Interest Subsidy Scheme: 6-7% (max ₹20-50 lakh/year for 7 years) for textile MSMEs/machine manufacturers, depending on block. Valid till 2025.
  • Capital Investment Subsidy: 30-50% of FCI (max ₹2-5 Cr for MSMEs, higher for large/anchor units), plus 5% extra for Women/SC/PADMA clusters. Valid till 2025.
  • Investment Subsidy in lieu of Net SGST: 25-50% of Net SGST for 8-10 years (cap 100-150% of FCI), depending on block/unit type. Valid till 2025.
  • Support for Fire Insurance Policy: 75% reimbursement (max ₹5-10 lakh/year) for fire insurance premium. Valid till 2025.
  • Skill Training Scheme: 50% subsidy (max ₹2-10 Cr) for setting up training centers in textile/apparel sectors. Valid till 2025.

III. Programme to Accelerate Development for MSME Advancement (PADMA)

PADMA is a 5-year policy (from Aug 2023) to set up new MSME clusters, attract investment, and create jobs in Haryana.

  • PADMA Cluster Infrastructure Development Scheme (PCIDS): 50-85% grant (max ₹35-45 Cr) for infrastructure in PADMA clusters (min 15-25 acres). No land purchase.
  • PADMA Capital Investment Subsidy: 35% (max ₹35 lakh) for Women/SC/SHG units, 25% (max ₹25 lakh) for others inside clusters; half for units outside. Valid 5 years.
  • PADMA Entrepreneurship Acceleration Scheme: ₹5 lakh/start-up for early-stage capital in/around PADMA clusters. Valid 5 years.
  • PADMA Interest Subsidy Scheme: 6% (max ₹20 lakh/year inside, ₹10 lakh/year outside) for 5 years for micro/small units. Valid 5 years.
  • PADMA Designing, Branding, Marketing & Exports Promotion: 50% (max ₹10 lakh/year) for branding, marketing, and export promotion for MSEs. Valid 5 years.
