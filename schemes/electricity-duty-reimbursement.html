Frequently Asked Questions

What is electricity duty?

Electricity duty is a tax levied by the state government on electricity consumption. It is typically 5-15% of the electricity bill amount.

How is the reimbursement calculated?

The reimbursement is calculated as 100% of the electricity duty paid by the MSME unit for 7 years from the date of commercial production.

When will I receive the reimbursement?

Reimbursement is typically processed quarterly. You will receive the amount directly in your registered bank account.

Can I apply if my unit is in a rural area?

Yes, the scheme is applicable to all MSMEs in Haryana, including those in rural areas.
