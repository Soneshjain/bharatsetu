Haryana State Scheme

Refund of Stamp Duty

Refund of eligible stamp duty on purchase/leasing of land for new MSEs.

Apply Now Check Eligibility

Objective

Refund of eligible stamp duty on purchase/leasing of land for new MSEs.

Quantum of Assistance

Refund of stamp duty paid on land purchase/leasing based on block category classification.

Authority

Directorate of Industries, Haryana

Processing Time

3-6 months

Eligibility Criteria

✓ Must be registered as Micro, Small, or Medium enterprise under MSME Act
✓ Unit must be located in any district of Haryana
✓ Must have valid Udyam Registration Number
✓ Must have valid Haryana Udhyam Memorandum (HUM) Number
✓ Must be a new MSE (Micro and Small Enterprise)
✓ Must have purchased/leased land for industrial purpose
✓ Must have paid stamp duty on land transaction
✓ Must have valid sale deed/lease deed and mutation

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Stamp Duty Refund Scheme?

The scheme provides refund of eligible stamp duty on purchase/leasing of land for new MSEs in Haryana.

Who is eligible for this scheme?

New MSEs registered under the MSME Act, located in Haryana, with valid Udyam and HUM registration numbers, and who have purchased/leased land for industrial purpose are eligible.

What documents are required?

Required documents include registration documents (HUM and URC), legal documents (sale deed, NOC/CLU), and financial documents (first sale bill).

How long does the processing take?

The application processing typically takes 3-6 months from the date of submission.
