Frequently Asked Questions

How is block type determined?

Block type is determined by the Haryana government based on the development level and infrastructure of the area. D blocks are rural/backward, C blocks are developing, and A/B blocks are urban/developed areas.

What is the time limit for land purchase?

Land must be purchased within 5 years from the date of starting commercial production. This is calculated from the date mentioned in your commercial production certificate.

Can I apply for multiple land purchases?

Yes, you can apply for stamp duty refund for multiple land purchases, but each must meet the eligibility criteria and be within the 5-year window.

How long does the refund process take?

The refund process typically takes 3-6 months from the date of application submission, depending on the completeness of documents and verification process.
