Frequently Asked Questions

How is connected load calculated?

Connected load is the total capacity of all electrical equipment installed in your unit, measured in Kilowatts (KW). It is determined by the electricity department based on your sanctioned load.

What is the difference between rural and urban subsidy?

Rural areas get higher maximum subsidy (₹20 lakhs) compared to urban areas (₹15 lakhs) to promote industrialization in rural areas. The per KW rate remains the same at ₹5,000.

Can I apply if I have multiple units?

Yes, you can apply for each unit separately, but each unit must meet the eligibility criteria and have separate MSME registrations.

How is the subsidy amount disbursed?

The subsidy is typically disbursed in installments over 2-3 years, directly to your registered bank account. The amount is calculated based on your actual electricity consumption.
