Haryana State Scheme

State Mini Revamped Scheme Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (Mini-SFURTI)

Cluster development and common facility center establishment for rural/traditional sectors.

Apply Now Check Eligibility

Objective

Cluster development and common facility center establishment for rural/traditional sectors.

Quantum of Assistance

Support for cluster development and common facility centers.

Authority

Directorate of Industries, Haryana

Processing Time

6-12 months

Eligibility Criteria

✓ Must be part of a traditional industry cluster
✓ Cluster must be located in Haryana
✓ Must be registered as Micro, Small, or Medium enterprise under MSME Act
✓ Must have valid Udyam Registration Number
✓ Must be part of SPV/Association
✓ Cluster must have minimum number of units/artisans
✓ Must be in traditional craft/industry sector

Technical Documents

  • Detailed Project Report (DPR) - Comprehensive project report for cluster development
  • Diagnostic Study Report (DSR) - Diagnostic study report of the traditional cluster

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Mini-SFURTI Scheme?

The Mini-SFURTI scheme focuses on cluster development and common facility center establishment for rural/traditional sectors.

Who is eligible for this scheme?

Traditional industry clusters located in Haryana, with registered MSMEs, part of SPV/Association, and in traditional craft/industry sectors are eligible.

What documents are required?

Required documents include legal documents (SPV member list, land documents), registration documents (artisan/weaver registration, HUM and URC details), and technical documents (DPR, DSR).

How long does the processing take?

The application processing typically takes 6-12 months from the date of submission.
