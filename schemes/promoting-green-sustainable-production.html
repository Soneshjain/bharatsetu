Eligible Sectors

Textile manufacturing units engaged in ginning & pressing, spinning, weaving, dyeing & processing, garment manufacturing, and related activities.

Timeline

Scheme effective from 19.12.2022 to 18.12.2025. Applications must be submitted within 4 months of installation.

Eligibility Criteria

Basic Requirements

  • Must be registered as Micro, Small, or Medium enterprise under MSME Act, 2006
  • Must have filed Udhyam Registration Certificate (URC) and Haryana Udhyam Memorandum (HUM)
  • Must be in commercial production (subsidy not released to closed units)
  • Must be a textile manufacturing unit engaged in value chain activities

Technical Requirements

  • Must have obtained NOC/CLU from competent Authority, if applicable
  • Must comply with environmental regulations
  • Equipment must be purchased and installed between 19.12.2022 and 18.12.2025
  • Must not be availing similar incentives under HEEP 2020

Textile Sector Activities

  • Ginning & Pressing
  • Spinning and Recycled PSF manufacturing
  • Weaving, Tufting, and Knitting
  • Dyeing & Processing
  • Garment/Made-ups/Apparels
  • Technical textiles and Machine manufacturing

Application Process

1

Prepare Documents

Gather all required documents including URC, HUM, CA certificate, invoices, and technical certificates.

2

Submit Application

Submit application on prescribed Form (Annexure-1) with all documents to Director General, Directorate of MSME within 4 months of installation.

3

Processing & Review

Application will be processed and examined at Directorate level. Deficiencies, if any, will be communicated within 7 days.

4

Approval & Disbursal

State Level Steering Committee will evaluate and approve. Grant will be released after complete installation and verification.

Required Documents

Registration Documents

  • Udhyam Registration Certificate (URC)
  • Haryana Udhyam Memorandum (HUM)
  • Certificate of Incorporation/Partnership deed
  • Board resolution/Power of attorney

Financial Documents

  • CA certificate of details of Expenditure incurred (original)
  • Copy of invoices and payment receipts
  • Copy of GST return/Audited Balance Sheet
  • Bank account statements

Technical Documents

  • Installation/completion certificate from empanelled vendor
  • Certificate from Regional Officer of Haryana Pollution Control Board
  • Change of Land Use (CLU)/NOC from competent authority
  • Detailed Project Report (DPR) - for ZLD only

Legal Documents

  • Declaration form (not obtained/applied for other subsidies)
  • Land title documents (sale deed or registered lease deed)
  • Memorandum and Articles of Association (if applicable)
  • Quotations from suppliers/vendors

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum subsidy available under this scheme?

+

The maximum subsidy varies by project type:

  • ETP: Up to ‚Çπ1 Crore (70-75% based on block category)
  • ZLD: Up to ‚Çπ10 Crore (40-75% based on block category)
  • Solar Rooftop: Up to ‚Çπ50 Lakh (30-50% based on block category)

How long does the application process take?

+

The application process typically takes 3-6 months from submission to approval. Applications must be submitted within 4 months of equipment installation or from the date of scheme notification, whichever is later.

Can I apply for multiple types of support under this scheme?

+

Yes, you can apply for ETP, ZLD, and Solar Rooftop support under the same scheme. However, you cannot avail similar incentives under HEEP 2020 simultaneously.

What are the block categories and how are they determined?

+

Block categories (A, B, C, D) are determined by the Haryana government based on development indicators. Higher subsidies are provided to less developed areas (C and D categories) to promote industrial development.

Is there any penalty for false information?

+

Yes, if false information is found, the applicant must refund the assistance with compound interest @ 12% per annum, face legal action, and be debarred from future incentives. The amount will be recovered as arrear of land revenue.
