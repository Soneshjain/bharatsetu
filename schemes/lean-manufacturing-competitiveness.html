Haryana State Scheme

State Mini Lean Manufacturing Competitiveness Scheme

To implement lean manufacturing practices in MSME mini-clusters for cost and quality improvements.

Apply Now Check Eligibility

Objective

To implement lean manufacturing practices in MSME mini-clusters for cost and quality improvements.

Quantum of Assistance

Support for lean manufacturing implementation and cluster development.

Authority

Directorate of Industries, Haryana

Processing Time

4-8 months

Eligibility Criteria

✓ Must be registered as Micro or Small enterprise under MSME Act
✓ Unit must be located in any district of Haryana
✓ Must have valid Udyam Registration Number
✓ Must have valid Haryana Udhyam Memorandum (HUM) Number
✓ Must be part of a mini-cluster (up to 12 members)
✓ Must be willing to implement lean manufacturing practices
✓ Must be in commercial production

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Lean Manufacturing Competitiveness Scheme?

The scheme implements lean manufacturing practices in MSME mini-clusters for cost and quality improvements.

Who is eligible for this scheme?

Micro and Small enterprises registered under the MSME Act, located in Haryana, and part of a mini-cluster (up to 12 members) are eligible.

What documents are required?

Required documents include legal documents (MoU, Power of Attorney), registration documents (HUM and Udyam certificates), and technical documents (diagnostic study reports).

How long does the processing take?

The application processing typically takes 4-8 months from the date of submission.
