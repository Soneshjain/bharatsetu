Haryana State Scheme

Promotion of Information & Communication Technology (ICT) Scheme

Support for adoption of cloud-based ERP solutions by MSMEs in Haryana to enhance digital transformation and operational efficiency.

Apply Now Check Eligibility

Objective

Support for adoption of cloud-based ERP solutions by MSMEs in Haryana to enhance digital transformation and operational efficiency.

Quantum of Assistance

Financial support for ERP subscription costs and implementation.

Authority

Directorate of Industries, Haryana

Processing Time

3-6 months

Eligibility Criteria

✓ Must be registered as Micro, Small, or Medium enterprise under MSME Act
✓ Unit must be located in any district of Haryana
✓ Must have valid Udyam Registration Number
✓ Must have valid Haryana Udhyam Memorandum (HUM) Number
✓ Must have subscribed to cloud-based ERP solution
✓ Must be in commercial production

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ICT Promotion Scheme?

The ICT Promotion Scheme provides financial support to MSMEs in Haryana for adopting cloud-based ERP solutions to enhance their digital transformation and operational efficiency.

Who is eligible for this scheme?

MSMEs registered under the MSME Act, located in Haryana, with valid Udyam and HUM registration numbers, and who have subscribed to cloud-based ERP solutions are eligible.

What documents are required?

Required documents include HUM and Udyam certificates, legal documents (incorporation/partnership deed, board resolution), financial documents (CA certificate, invoices), and technical documents (ERP agreement).

How long does the processing take?

The application processing typically takes 3-6 months from the date of submission.
