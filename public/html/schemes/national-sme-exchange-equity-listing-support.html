Haryana State Scheme

SME Exchange Equity Scheme

To facilitate SMEs in raising capital via SME exchanges (NSE/BSE Emerge) for business expansion and growth.

Apply Now Check Eligibility

Objective

To facilitate SMEs in raising capital via SME exchanges (NSE/BSE Emerge) for business expansion and growth.

Quantum of Assistance

Support for listing on SME exchanges and capital market access.

Authority

Directorate of Industries, Haryana

Processing Time

6-12 months

Eligibility Criteria

✓ Must be registered as Micro, Small, or Medium enterprise under MSME Act
✓ Unit must be located in any district of Haryana
✓ Must have valid Udyam Registration Number
✓ Must have valid Haryana Udhyam Memorandum (HUM) Number
✓ Must be seeking listing on SME exchanges (NSE/BSE Emerge)
✓ Must have commenced commercial production
✓ Must have valid GST registration

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the SME Exchange Equity Scheme?

The SME Exchange Equity Scheme facilitates SMEs in raising capital via SME exchanges (NSE/BSE Emerge) for business expansion and growth.

Who is eligible for this scheme?

MSMEs registered under the MSME Act, located in Haryana, with valid Udyam and HUM registration numbers, and seeking listing on SME exchanges are eligible.

What documents are required?

Required documents include legal documents (incorporation/partnership deed, board resolution), registration documents (HUM and Udyam certificates), and financial documents (CA certificate, audited balance sheet).

How long does the processing take?

The application processing typically takes 6-12 months from the date of submission.
