Haryana State Scheme

Reimbursement of Electricity Duty

To reduce initial financial burden on new MSMEs by reimbursing electricity duty for 7 years.

Apply Now Check Eligibility

Objective

To reduce initial financial burden on new MSMEs by reimbursing electricity duty for 7 years.

Quantum of Assistance

100% reimbursement of electricity duty for 7 years from the date of commercial production.

Authority

Directorate of Industries, Haryana

Processing Time

3-6 months

Eligibility Criteria

✓ Must be registered as Micro, Small, or Medium enterprise under MSME Act
✓ Unit must be located in any district of Haryana
✓ Must have valid Udyam Registration Number
✓ Must have valid Haryana Udhyam Memorandum (HUM) Number
✓ Must have started commercial production after policy implementation
✓ Must have valid electricity connection and be paying electricity duty
✓ Must not be in restrictive/negative list

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Electricity Duty Reimbursement Scheme?

The scheme reduces initial financial burden on new MSMEs by reimbursing electricity duty for 7 years from the date of commercial production.

Who is eligible for this scheme?

MSMEs registered under the MSME Act, located in Haryana, with valid Udyam and HUM registration numbers, and paying electricity duty are eligible.

What documents are required?

Required documents include registration documents (HUM and Udyam certificates), financial documents (CA certificate, first sale bill), legal documents (declaration, commercial production certificate), and technical documents (electricity connection details).

How long does the processing take?

The application processing typically takes 3-6 months from the date of submission.
